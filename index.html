About College

ALNM'S Vyshnavi Women's Degree College, Kanigiri is committed to providing quality higher education for women, nurturing academic excellence, discipline, leadership and social responsibility.

College Information

ALNM'S Vyshnavi Women's Degree College, Kanigiri was established in the year 2015 with a noble vision of providing quality higher education to women and empowering them academically, socially, and professionally.

Since its inception, the college has successfully guided and supported hundreds of girl students in completing their undergraduate degree education, helping them build strong careers and responsible futures.

The college is well-known for its disciplined academic environment, excellent teaching standards, and student-focused learning approach. Special emphasis is given to conceptual understanding, practical knowledge, and moral values.

Highly qualified and experienced faculty members form the backbone of the institution. The staff is friendly, supportive, and committed to guiding students both academically and personally.

The campus is equipped with a good and well-maintained college building, spacious classrooms, peaceful surroundings, and essential facilities that create a positive and safe learning atmosphere for women students.
